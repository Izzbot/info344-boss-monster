INSTRUCTIONS

YouTag is a tagging service for Youtube videos. It allows you to quickly tag videos into groups so you can have organized views of related videos easily.

You must log in with your assigned username and password to use YouTag.

To tag a new video:

  • Click on the plus sign and enter a url and a list of tags.
    • url - a youtube video URL.
    • tags - a comma-separated list of tags you want to associate with this video. No spaces are allowed in tags.
  • Click on the Save button.

To see a list of videos associated with a particular tag:

  • Click on Tags in the menu.
  • Click on the button with the tag you want to view.

To delete a tagged video:

  • Click on Videos in the menu.
  • Click on the title of the video you wish to delete.
  • Click on the red X in the upper right to delete the video.

API Reference

There is one accessible API endpoint, (site)/final/api. You may choose to indicate the primary key of a particular TagVideo object, or you can leave it blank.

If you access it with a primary key, you may GET, PUT, or DELETE.

  1. GET - gives you the details and the primary keys of related Tags and the related Video object.
  2. PUT* - lets you edit the details of the TagVideo object by providing a properly formed JSON object.
  3. DELETE* - lets you delete your TagVideo object.

If you access it without a primary key provided, you may GET or POST.

  1. GET* - gives you a list and the details of all your created TagVideo objects.
  2. POST* - Requires a custom JSON object. Two string values should be provided for the keys "url" and "tags".
    • url - a youtube video URL.
    • tags - a comma-separated list of tags you want to associate with this video. No spaces are allowed in tags.
